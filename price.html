Major Cost Components

✈️ • Flights: • Round‑trip Santiago → Easter Island (LATAM Airlines) usually $500–$900 depending on season. • International flight to Santiago from Europe adds $900–$1,400 on average. • Accommodation:
• Budget guesthouses: $40–$60 per night. • Mid‑range hotels: $80–$150 per night. • High‑end resorts: $200+ per night. • Food & Dining:
• Average daily food cost: $30–$40 per person. • Restaurants are relatively expensive due to island imports. • Activities & Tours:
• National Park entry fee: $80 per adult (valid for 10 days). • Guided tours: $50–$100 per day.
• Car rental: $60–$80 per day.
